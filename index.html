Colour

  • Cool Beige: hsl(30, 100%, 96%)
  • Peachy Orange: hsl(30, 100%, 83%)
  • Musty Grey: hsl(30, 12%, 77%)
  • Silver Ash: hsl(27, 4%, 54%)
  • Smoke: hsl(30, 3%, 34%)

Light colours (i.e. Cool Beige or Peachy Orange) serve as a background or contrast text to the main sections or banners. Smoke provides the main text colour, with Musty Grey and Silver Ash providing colours for smaller elements and details such as navigation banners and borders.
